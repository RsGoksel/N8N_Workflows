## Why Some Workflows Sell Better Than Others

Pattern across top-sellers:

1. **Direct ROI story** — buyer can quantify ROI in 30 sec ("recovers your payment terms" beats "saves time")
2. **Demoable visually** — a screenshot of the actual output (email, dashboard, Telegram alert) closes more sales than a feature list
3. **Specific niche, not generic** — "AI email outreach for SaaS founders" sells better than "AI email automation"
4. **Recurring pain** — daily/weekly tasks, not one-off
5. **Easy to verify** — buyer can run it and see results within 5 minutes
6. **Status / identity signal** — buyer feels "smart" using it (signal bots, dashboards)

## The Repo Strategy

Goal: **Become the "n8n workflows for finance & lead gen" GitHub destination.**

Plan:
1. Build 3 finance-focused workflows in 1 week (Daily Stock Brief ✓, Crypto Signals, Portfolio Risk Alert)
2. Build 1 lead-gen workflow (different vertical, broader market)
3. Each workflow free on GitHub (MIT) — drives stars/visibility

5. Upsell: Done-for-you customization ($299 for custom watchlists, $999 for custom integrations)

**Why free + premium dual:**

- Gumroad bundle = casual buyers who want plug-and-play
- DFY upsell = high-ticket, ~$1k AOV
